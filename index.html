How Vera works

Speak. Done.

No setup. No app switching. No copy-paste. Vera works at the interface level — across every device simultaneously.

Speak naturally. No commands to memorise. No special phrasing. Just tell Vera what you need — send this, create that, open this, summarise that — in your own words.
